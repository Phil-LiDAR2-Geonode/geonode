Launched on July 1, 2014, REMap is the 5th component of the Phil-LiDAR 2 Program and is a directed research funded by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST).

It explores the utilization of LiDAR and other geospatial data for evaluating renewable energy resources, focusing on biomass, hydro, solar, and wind.


The objectives of the Project are:
1. Develop workflows and algorithms for RE resource assessment using LiDAR and other geospatial data and technologies;
2. Assess the renewable energy potential of the Philippines for biomass, hydro, solar, and wind; and
3. Perform site suitability assessment for the development of biomass, hydro, solar, and wind.
